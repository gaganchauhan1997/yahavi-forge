3 steps · 60 seconds · ₹0
STEP 1

Grab a free API key

Open console.groq.com/keys — sign up with email (no card), click Create Key, copy the gsk_... string. Takes 60 seconds.

▸ Groq is fastest. Gemini gives you 1M context. OpenRouter routes to 100+ models. All free tier — keep all three.

STEP 2

Paste it in ▸ KEYS

Open Yahavi Forge, click ▸ KEYS (bottom of the sidebar). Paste your key into the Groq field. Save. A green dot appears in the nav.

▸ Multiple keys = automatic fallback when one rate-limits. Forge routes to whichever has capacity.

STEP 3

Use 17 tools, instantly

Open Resume Builder, paste your bullets, pick a tone, hit RUN. Or run ATS Checker on your resume vs a JD — get a 0–100 score instantly (no key needed for the local scorer). Truth-Lock Tailor. Roast. Cover Letter. Interview Prep. All 17.
