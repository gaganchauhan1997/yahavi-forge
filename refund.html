Yahavi Forge offers a free tier and optional paid plans (Day Pass ₹49, Monthly ₹249, Yearly ₹2,499) processed via Razorpay. Because our paid plans deliver instant digital access, our default policy is no refunds once access is activated, with the exceptions listed below. Please read before purchasing.

01Free tier

No payment is taken for the free tier. There is nothing to refund or cancel. You may stop using Forge at any time.

02Paid plans — general policy

Paid plans grant immediate digital access to Forge tools. Because access is delivered instantly and the service is used as you proceed, purchases are generally non-refundable once access is activated. This is consistent with the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules 2020 and standard practice for instantly-delivered digital services in India.

03Refund exceptions

We will issue a full or pro-rata refund in these circumstances:

  • Access not delivered — payment confirmed by Razorpay but tools not unlocked within 15 minutes due to a Forge error. Contact us within 7 days with your Razorpay payment ID.
  • Duplicate payment — if you were charged more than once for the same plan in the same session due to a technical error.
  • Service discontinuation — if Hackknow permanently shuts down Yahavi Forge, you will receive a pro-rata refund for any unused paid period.
  • Fraud / unauthorised transaction — if your payment method was used without your authorisation, raise a dispute with Razorpay and contact us simultaneously.

04How to request a refund

  1. Email team@hackknow.com with the subject line "Refund Request — [your Razorpay payment ID]".
  2. Include: your payment ID (from the Razorpay receipt email), the plan purchased, the date of purchase, and a brief description of the issue.
  3. We will acknowledge within 2 business days and, if approved, process the refund within 7 business days. Refunds are returned to your original payment method via Razorpay.
▸ Note Refunds are processed through Razorpay back to the original payment method. Bank/card processing times vary (typically 5–10 business days after we initiate the refund).

05Cancellation

Forge plans are not auto-renewing subscriptions — you pay once and access lasts for the plan period (24 hours / 30 days / 365 days). There is nothing to "cancel". Your access simply expires at the end of the period unless you purchase a new plan.

If you wish to delete your HackKnow account, see the account-deletion process in our Privacy Policy.

06Payment processor — Razorpay

All payments are processed by Razorpay Software Private Limited. Your payment details (card number, UPI ID, etc.) are handled directly by Razorpay and are not stored by Hackknow. For Razorpay-related disputes or failed-transaction issues, you may also raise a dispute directly with Razorpay.
