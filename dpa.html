Data Processing
Addendum

Service: Yahavi Forge Effective: 26 June 2026 Version: 1.0
This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the Terms & Conditions between Hackknow ("we") and any customer or organisation that uses Yahavi Forge to process personal data ("Customer", "you"). It reflects the requirements of India's DPDP Act 2023 and, where applicable, the GDPR. For most individual users no DPA is needed — Forge stores keys and resume text only in your browser; this DPA matters mainly for business/team use.

01Roles & scope

For account and authentication data, Hackknow acts as a controller / data fiduciary. To the limited extent you direct Forge to process personal data on your behalf, Hackknow acts as a processor / data processor and will process such data only on your documented instructions and to provide the service.

▸ Key factThe most sensitive data (your API keys and the resume/job text you enter) is processed in your browser and sent directly to the AI provider you choose. Hackknow does not receive or store that content on its servers.

02Nature of processing

  • Subject matter: providing the Forge AI career tools and HackKnow account/SSO.
  • Categories of data: account identifiers (email, name, avatar, provider id), authentication/usage metadata; any content you choose to enter (processed client-side).
  • Data subjects: you and end users of your account.
  • Duration: for the term of your use of Forge, subject to the retention terms in the Privacy Policy.

04Security measures

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures: HTTPS/TLS in transit, row-level security on our database, least-privilege access, a client-side key model that keeps secrets off our servers, and reputable processors. No system is perfectly secure, but minimising what we hold is itself a core safeguard.

05Data subject rights & assistance

We will provide reasonable assistance to help you respond to data subject requests (access, correction, deletion, portability, objection) and to meet your security, breach-notification, and impact-assessment obligations, taking into account the nature of the processing and the information available to us.

06Breach notification

If we become aware of a personal-data breach affecting data processed for you, we will notify you without undue delay and provide the information reasonably needed for you to meet your reporting duties.

07International transfers

Where data is transferred across borders (e.g. our database in Singapore, global processors), we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms required by applicable law.

08Return & deletion

On termination, or on your request, we will delete or return personal data processed on your behalf, except where retention is required by law. Account data deletion is described in the Privacy Policy.
