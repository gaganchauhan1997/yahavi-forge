Forge uses almost no cookies. We don't run advertising or cross-site tracking. The little we store lives in your browser to make the app work. This page explains exactly what and why, including all localStorage keys set by the app. It supplements our Privacy Policy.

02Third-party storage

Supabase — if you sign in, sets essential authentication storage to keep you logged in across visits.

Razorpay — if you purchase a paid plan, Razorpay's checkout script (checkout.razorpay.com/v1/checkout.js) is loaded and may set its own storage for fraud-prevention and UX (e.g. remembering your UPI ID). This is governed by Razorpay's Privacy Policy. Razorpay does not store your full card number on our site — only in their PCI-DSS-compliant systems.

Google Fonts & CDN — loading fonts from Google Fonts and scripts from jsDelivr CDN sends standard request data (IP, user-agent) to those providers as part of delivering the files.

03Managing & clearing

You can clear all Forge storage at any time:

  • In-app Keys panel — removes your API keys
  • Settings → Clear All — removes keys, drafts, history, uploads, prefs, session, and review data from this device
  • Browser site data — clear via your browser's Developer Tools or Privacy settings

Clearing storage signs you out and removes your saved keys and preferences from this device. Blocking storage entirely may prevent the app from remembering your keys between visits.
