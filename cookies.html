02Third-party storage

When you sign in, Supabase sets essential authentication storage to keep you logged in. When the page loads supabase-js from a CDN (jsDelivr) and fonts from Google Fonts, those providers may receive standard request data (e.g. IP, user-agent) as part of serving the files. The AI providers you connect receive only what your browser sends them when you run a tool.

03Managing & clearing

You can clear your keys and preferences any time from the in-app ▸ KEYS panel, or by clearing site data in your browser settings. Clearing storage signs you out and removes your saved keys/prefs from this device. Blocking storage entirely may stop the app from remembering your keys between visits.
